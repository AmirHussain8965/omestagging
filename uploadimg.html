About Us

OME Staging was founded by Jacinthe Leroux who has earned the reputation of being one of Quebec’s most renown Interior designers.

The goal of Ome Staging is to create affordable detailed staging plans for every room in the house. Jacinthe’s team of expert designers will analyze the photos of each home based on the unique architectural style and existing furnishings. The design team then creates virtual staging images with the goal of maximizing the look and feel of each home.

Utilizing design expertise, the latest technology in photo augmentation and the tasteful use of Artificial Intelligence, the OME Staging team will create a photo image and shopping list of what small improvements can be made to your home to make a huge difference in appealing to potential buyers.Ome Staging has created a platform that is unique in the home staging market. We can deliver a customized plan for every room along with a shopping list. Staging your home to sell has never been easier or more affordable.
